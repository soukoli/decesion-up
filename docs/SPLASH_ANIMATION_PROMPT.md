# Nana Banana Prompt - DecisionUp Splash Screen Animation

## Context
Creating a splash screen animation for DecisionUp - a personal intelligence feed app. The app aggregates news, trends, stocks, and AI research into one mobile-first dashboard.

## Reference
Inspiration: https://dribbble.com/shots/26777932-Endorphins-Running-App-Interactions

## Logo Description
The logo is a stylized abstract icon representing "decision" and "upward movement" - modern, geometric design in gold/amber (#d4a84b) tones on a dark background.

## Animation Brief

### Scene 1: Emergence (0-800ms)
- Dark navy background (#0f172a) with subtle gradient pulse
- Logo emerges from center with elastic bounce effect
- Subtle particles radiate outward from logo
- Golden glow builds around the logo

### Scene 2: Pulse & Energy (800-1800ms)
- Logo pulses with breathing glow effect
- Concentric rings expand outward (like radar pulse)
- Small energy particles orbit around logo
- App name "DecisionUp" fades in below with stagger animation
- Tagline "Signal, not noise" appears with typewriter or fade effect

### Scene 3: Transition (1800-2500ms)
- Logo scales up slightly (1.1x)
- Everything fades to white or zooms into logo
- Seamless transition to main app

## Technical Specs
- Duration: 2.5 seconds
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (mobile portrait)
- Format: Lottie JSON or GIF sequence
- Colors: 
  - Background: #0f172a (navy), #020617 (darker)
  - Accent: #d4a84b (gold/amber)
  - Text: #ffffff (white)
  - Glow: rgba(212,168,75,0.5)

## Style Keywords
- Premium, Minimal, Modern
- Subtle motion, not overwhelming
- Smooth easing curves
- Professional, trustworthy feel
- Similar energy to Calm app or premium fintech apps
